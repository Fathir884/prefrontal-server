# Privacy Policy for Prefrontal

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-20

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Prefrontal**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our application and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

## 2. Data We Collect
We collect the following types of information:
*   **Personal Information:** Name (optional, stored locally), Goals.
*   **Usage Data:** Task completion stats, pomodoro timer logs, financial entries.
*   **AI Interactions:** Messages sent to the AI assistant (processed via Google Gemini API).

## 3. How We Use Your Data
*   To provide and maintain the App.
*   To generate AI responses (your text is sent to Google's Gemini API but not stored on our external servers permanently).
*   To provide productivity analytics.

## 4. Data Storage
**Local Storage:** most of your data (journals, tasks, finance) is stored locally on your device using `localStorage`.
**Cloud Processing:** Text sent to the AI is processed by Google Cloud but not retained by us for training.

## 5. Permissions
*   **Notifications:** Used for Pomodoro timer and reminders.
*   **Storage/Audio:** Used for generating PDF reports and playing focus sounds.
